002

Research · Embodied AI

ZSON + SG-Nav

Zero-shot indoor object navigation with a memory-augmented agent. Identified core LLM bottleneck (no memory/feedback), designed agent framework with memory modules and floor-level context injection. Improved success rate 36% → 40% on HM3D.

Habitat LLaMA 3.2 CLIP Embodied AI

Research · RL

MuZero Chess

MuZero architecture for chess self-play. Analyzes design trade-offs of learned world models (representation, dynamics, prediction) vs. model-free RL. Quantifies planning depth vs. agent decision performance.

PyTorch MCTS RL World Models

Research · RAG

Bilingual drug interaction Q&A system built on DrugBank 6.0. Compares GraphRAG (Neo4j + Cypher) vs. Flat RAG (Qdrant + BGE-M3) on multi-hop reasoning. Includes a 500-question stratified benchmark across 1/2/3-hop complexity.
